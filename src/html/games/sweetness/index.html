Sweetness

Well, a very beautiful mobile game.

Sweetness is an exciting Mobile Game in which you play as a little explorer who travels around the big and to an unknown world to find the sweetest sugar.

Your hero is a real adventurer, ready to explore all corners of this a magical land to find a real paradise for a sweet tooth. Him you will have to overcome many obstacles, bypass traps and defeat all enemies on your way. But when will he finally find his valuable trophy, all this will pay off!

The game will allow you to go through an exciting journey through the world of various locations – from forests and deserts to cities and ancient castles. Each the location will have its own characteristics, and many different obstacles, funny characters and mysterious secrets will not allow you'll be bored.

In Sweetness you will find many recognizable elements from other games: collect coins, dodge traps and overcome obstacles. But what What makes this game so special is that the hero is a little explorer with his weakness for sweets. He can pass levels thanks to your love of sugar – collecting candy and sweets on your way. Except in addition, on his way he can discover various chocolate coins, which will help him to acquire additional skills and overcome difficulties.

Sweetness game is created for those who like to test their abilities, take risks and find new adventures along the way. This the perfect game for Everyone who is ready with new strength and with a huge love to continue your sugar adventures.
