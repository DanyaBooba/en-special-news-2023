Have you ever wondered how interesting it would be to go to traveling to Russia with your grandmother? Now you have one opportunity – in the mobile game "Babka On The Hunt"!

In this exciting game you will play as a grandmother who goes on a trip to different locations in Russia. You will have to visit places such as Pavlovsky Posad, hospital, grandmother's house, the planet Exolon and even the Lyceum of MIPT im. Kapitsa!
