About the Special project

What is Special?

Special is an open source of media sites dybka.ru, it.dybka.ru, creagoo.ru and others, owned by Daniil Dybka (dybka.ru).

The main task of the Special website is simple language and convenient ways to convey useful information to people. The Special website provides convenient access to reading articles. Choosing on which platform to produce the reading is behind you.

Become an Author

You have the opportunity to offer an interesting article for the Special website. To add you as an Author on the Special website, it is enough contact the mailing address daniil@dybka.ru.

You should attach the following materials to the application for the postal address: the name of the post, the content of the post (in the content, specify what is text, and what is the name), a photo of the post.

You will also need to specify your first name, last name and desired avatar (128x128), which will be displayed on your article author page.

Your application will be reviewed. In case of positive verification, your post will be published on the Special website.

Forbidden topics

It is forbidden to post materials with profanity on the Special website . vocabulary, insults, incitement to hatred. For discussion prohibited: topics of war, topics of religion, "acute" topics and others that they can cause general negativity in people.

Advertising posts are prohibited for publication without the approval of moderators on their publication. Any presence of advertising displays and other things will be be the reason for the refusal to publish the material.

All published posts are checked by moderators. To publish a post without the consent of the moderators is not possible.

Advertising on the site

On the Media Special website, you have the opportunity to order an advertisement. You should contact the mailing address daniil@dybka.ru.

Contact us at the postal address, describe your offer. Also specify the format in which you want to see the ad. If approved advertising publications, Media Special will fulfill your requirements for advertising publications on the Media Special website.

It is forbidden to advertise

All topics that are prohibited in the territory are prohibited from advertising RF. In the case of Media Special prohibited for advertising: prohibited funds, 18+ products, various casinos, betting companies, platforms based on deception of users.

In the event that your product does not have goals to deceive the audience, to make money on this and is not prohibited on the territory of the Russian Federation and on on the Special website, then you can count on the publication of advertising, in case of interest from both sides (yours and the Special media).

Site security

The domain address uses a secure connection protocol. An SSL certificate is used for secure and fast connection and getting information. The site pages are formed before publication and are "static".

Technical optimization

A lot of attention was paid to active optimization site loading, accessibility of content from the keyboard, adaptive displaying the site on different devices, the opportunity to get acquainted with content under different conditions.

The above criteria are not mandatory, it is a fact. There are only "recommendations" in creating a website. But there are also differences between a good site and a bad one. I don't want to use a bad website, then what is the point of this site if the user does not want to receive information from the site?

Attention is actively paid to the user. There is a large number of people who test the display of the site. These are really important factors that help users stay on the site longer.

Site availability

The Special site is available to All users. There is a possibility navigate through the elements using the keyboard and access about information about interactive elements. The possibility is configured using programs to read text from the screen. Registered parameters are descriptions for images so that all people can find out what the picture posted on the site tells us.
