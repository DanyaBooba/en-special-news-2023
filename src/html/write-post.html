Any user has the opportunity to get his published article on the website news.dybka.ru , if the publication rules are followed, which will be discussed further.

Become an Author

You should write an article with a title (title), a short description, the main content (text) and an image.

Post format:

  • The title (title) should not exceed 90-one hundred characters and required.
  • The short description must not exceed 42 characters and must not necessarily.
  • The image of the recording should be 1200:600 resolution and the ratio sides 2:1, respectively, must be recorded.
  • The text of the record can be any size, it is mandatory to record.

How do I publish an article?

To publish Articles, you should send the attached material to the letter to the post office: daniil@dybka.ru. The attached article will be reviewed, after consideration you will be a reply has been sent.

Forbidden topics

It is forbidden to post materials with profanity on the Special website . vocabulary, insults, incitement to hatred. For discussion prohibited: topics of war, topics of religion, "acute" topics and others that they can cause general negativity in people.

Advertising posts are prohibited for publication without the approval of moderators on their publication. Any presence of advertising displays and other things will be be the reason for the refusal to publish the material.

All published posts are checked by moderators. To publish a post without the consent of the moderators is not possible.

Author's page

If your article has been approved for publication, it will be created for you the Author page. You will be asked to send your photo, which will be attached to the Author's page. Important: the photo can only be Your face, other photos are not accepted. By default it will be there is a photo with your initials: first name and last name.
