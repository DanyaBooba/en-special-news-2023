I had no problems with the battery. Device it is comfortable to use and solve problems without necessarily connecting the macbook to the power source.

Beautiful macOS, about which I said a lot of good things already above.

Excellent optimization of programs, which will fully allow get comfort: mount, program, write text, do graphics and other things.

Incredible integration with the Apple ecosystem, which allows me connect my devices into a single communication, exchange data, use seamless dispatch of materials and not experience any discomfort in the issue of sending data.

Problems of this MacBook

As mentioned above — this MacBook has been on the market for 8 years and buy a brand new device is not possible, with a huge the probability is that you will buy a once-used device, so called used.

On my device, the battery was poorly glued to the case, so it hanging out. Fixed the moment very simply — I bought a two-sided scotch tape and well glued the battery to the case so that it no longer staggered in the case.

I replaced the thermal paste on the laptop for better performance and less heating.

There were also 3 bolts missing from the back of the lid. I ordered from the store bolts for your laptop model and successfully replaced them. Be be careful that you order bolts specifically for your laptop model. Note that some bolts are attached at an angle to the lid. And yet, 2 bolts will be of a smaller size — these two bolts need to be fixed in the side where the cooling hole is located.

All this was exactly on my device, when buying you can inspect the device and choose a device with a smaller number defects. By the way, I also have a desire to order legs for a laptop and replace them too.

And now about the model itself: since the production of this device a lot of time has passed, because of which the battery has lost a lot the amount of its capacity and it should be replaced.

The most terrible defect that exists on this model is the display. And it's not about its quality, no, the quality is incredible. And the layer that this display is covered. The display on this laptop is by no means you can't get wet. It should not get drops of water or any other liquids.

On my MacBook, I decided to stick a film on the display to avoid direct contact with a drop of water or any other liquid. If on this display will get liquid — the display will start to bubble and to make divorces that cannot be removed. So you too I recommend gluing the film. It does not distort the color and does not interfere with it work.

The only thing I would highlight is that the diagonal of 13 inches is too small for basically a workstation, and I was convinced of this in practice. Macbook incredibly convenient and comfortable. I can't part with him for a minute. a minute, but sometimes there is a desire to reduce the zoom interface or perform other operations to increase the number of content on the monitor.

I solve the problem with the diagonal by connecting an external monitor, on which I perform operations related to the code. That's why I'm in I remain fully satisfied and do not want to change anything.

Some time ago I was not so strong I am sure to buy a MacBook. I just wished for a light a device on which it will be convenient for me to solve my tasks.

I can't find a valid decent device on Windows it turned out, and I haven't been looking for so long. As a result, the choice fell on a macbook and in the end I bought it for myself.

As I said above, I needed a lightweight device for programming and other tasks. I often go on a "journey", I don't need a laptop with a 4090, just good technology is enough for me, which I will be pleased to use.

Sometimes I use a macbook at home. For example, I type text on it or do some work on it. I have a laptop that is in as the main working device to which I connect I use the keyboard and use an external monitor.

Conclusion

The article does not have a large technical part, it is aimed at answering the question "For whom a MacBook is best suited" and "Is it worth taking try it." Not everyone will have such an impression of the purchase devices, All depends on the person himself and his tasks.

Personally, I am absolutely happy to use a macbook for programming and other tasks. I can recommend it!
